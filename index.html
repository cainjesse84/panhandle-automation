    Common questions

    Before we connect anything.

    What kinds of tools can you connect?

    Common business apps, forms, email, calendars, spreadsheets, CRMs, accounting tools, and services with usable APIs. We confirm compatibility during the audit.

    Do I need to replace my current software?

    Usually not. The goal is to improve the tools you already use. If a tool cannot connect safely or reliably, we explain the options before recommending a change.

    Will AI make decisions for my business?

    Only where appropriate and agreed. Sensitive or high-impact steps should use rules, human approval, or both.

    What does the free audit include?

    A short review of one process, its likely automation opportunities, key dependencies, and a suggested next step. It is not a full technical implementation.

    Are results guaranteed?

    No. Savings and outcomes depend on the process, data quality, tool limitations, adoption, and other factors. We use estimates to guide decisions, then validate with testing.
